主机版指可在游戏主机上游玩的Minecraft，均由4J Studios开发。其适配：

Xbox 360
Xbox One
PlayStation 3
PlayStation 4
PlayStation Vita
Wii U


主机版引入了新的特性，比如更简单的合成系统，游戏内的教程，同屏幕内的多人游戏（除了PlayStation Vita），也有通过PlayStation Network（仅在PS4上需要PlayStation Plus会员）或Xbox Live（需要Xbox Live Gold会员）实现的最多8人同时在线的多人游戏。就像其他的游戏一样，后续更新会被免费提供。

游戏内容[编辑 | 编辑源代码]




Xbox 360版的一张游戏截图。


游戏的目的同原本的电脑版和携带版游戏一样，即玩家可以在沙盒的虚拟环境之下建造。这里没有预设的目标，玩家可以自己制定自己的目标。
在很多部分当中，主机版的游戏和电脑版游戏除了几个微小的差别之外几乎相同，[5]但一度当电脑版是 1.2.5的时候，Xbox 360版的内容相当于电脑版的Beta 1.6.6。
与PC版的区别[编辑 | 编辑源代码]

主条目： 主机版独有特性

与电脑版1.8的区别包括：

The game features splitscreen functionality,[6] however an HDTV with a Component or HDMI cable is required for split-screen play. Split-screen functionality works with a VGA connection to a PC monitor as well as HDMI-to-DVI cables to a PC monitor. Up to 4 players can play in split screen, as well as the possibility to play 8-person online multiplayer game modes in split-screen. The "PlayStation Vita Edition" only supports up to 2-4 players online and Ad-Hoc support.
The game includes an automatic 合成 system where you do not have to place the blocks in the grid. However, there is an option to use the PC crafting.
The Console Edition includes a tutorial mode not included in the PC version of the game. This tutorial leads the player through the basics of 挖掘 and placing 方块, 合成, as well as 造他们的新房子 to survive the 夜晚, 钓鱼, 附魔, 药水酿造, and more.[7]
Many blocks/items use outdated sounds.
在开始一个新世界时，玩家便会拿到一张地图。
For the Xbox 360, PlayStation 3, the PlayStation Vita and Wii U, the world size is limited to 864×864 blocks. Because the outer-most block is unusable on each side of the map, it is effectively 862×862 blocks that can be used. The worlds on the Xbox One and PlayStation 4 can be up to 5000×5000.
下界 is limited in size and because of this the movement distance in the Nether vs. the Overworld is 1:3 versus 1:8 in the PC version. Consequently the Nether is limited to about 288×288 for the PS3, PS4 and Wii U versions. 末路之地 also uses the aforementioned size.
Skins can only be chosen from those available as default or from downloadable content.
Breeding and utility mob spawning works the same as the PC version with the exception of limiting tamed 狼 and 雪傀儡 populations to 16. If the player attempts to tame or breed a 17th wolf, it will be killed by other wolves, and if a player attempts to build a 17th snow golem, upon placing the pumpkin all three blocks will break, dropping one pumpkin and eight snowballs. Also, wolf pups are born with full health and reach adulthood without player input.
The edge of the world cannot be mined and is completely sea. The edge of the map is a strip of water surrounding the map followed by an invisible barrier in which all players cannot gain access to. This can cause problems as sometimes chests in 废弃矿井 generate at the edge of the map, causing the player to make and place a new chest next to said chest in order to access all the previously inaccessible items.
Creative mode flying does not conserve momentum, meaning if you sprint and stop quickly, you will not drift to another block.
The 末影龙 has a different fighting style and when at low health will hover above the ground spewing out acid.
两个被铁栏杆保护的末影水晶。
当创建一个超平坦世界，下界也会是平坦的。
地狱疣随机在下界中的灵魂沙上生成，而在电脑版中，它只在下界要塞生成。
下界的边缘是基岩。
The PlayStation Vita Edition has Ad-Hoc support like an offline multiplayer service. It can be toggled on or off (Online or Ad-Hoc), and is also similar to LAN servers but without internet connection.
The Wii U version currently does not support leaderboards.

你知道吗[编辑 | 编辑源代码]

开发Minecraft主机版的公司4J Studios也只做其他几个电子游戏，如为Xbox Live Arcade制作的“班卓熊大冒险”“Banjo Tooie”，和“Perfect Dark”以及为PS3制作的“上古卷轴IV：湮没”
Notch说Xbox 360是Minecraft主机版唯一的提供商。[8]然而，索尼后来宣布会有PS3，PS4，和Vita的版本发布。[9][10]
没有办法在主机版上合法地安装Mod，但是可以破解去安装。注意：如果你试用Mod，你可能会被封禁。
